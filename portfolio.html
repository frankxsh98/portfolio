Interaction Design

Diet Me

Diet Me is an application to help users establish healthy eating habits and deal with overweight problems. It can evaluate their daily meals according to preset weight management goals.

Working Process

Problem Statements

Many people are facing overweight problems. Main causes of the problems include unhealthy diet, lack of exercise and lack of knowledge about personal physical condition. For this project, at first, I wanted to design an integrated product that takes all three aspects into account. As the design process goes on, I decided to narrow down the scope and focus on the diet management.

Competetive Analysis

There are many applications on market that deal with the problem from the three aspects mentioned above. Typical examples are MyFitnessPal (diet management), Keep (exercise instructions) and VeSyncFit (Body Index scale).

Idea Sketching

I came up with 8 different tools that can help solve the problems and visualize them by sketching.
